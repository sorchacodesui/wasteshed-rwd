Contributing to Classrooms

At the WasteShed we partner with surrounding Chicago schools support teachers in receiving the much-needed school supplies.

How can you make an impact? Begin by selecting a school to support. Each school has a wish list of supplies they would like— items can range from pencils to easels.

Once you've checked off the items from the list, schedule a time that works best for you to drop off your items at one of our WasteShed locations.

After you drop off your donated materials, we'll take it from there and bring the items to the school you're supporting.
