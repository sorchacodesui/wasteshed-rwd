Donations We Accept

Art Supplies

Paint (except house paint), dyes, brushes, ink, hot glue guns, glue, art tools, stencils, pencils, pens, markers, pastels, charcoal, ceramic tools and glazes, pencil sharpeners, mineral spirits, cutting mats, moldmaking and casting materials.

Fabric & Notions

Fabric yardage only (1/2 yard and larger), yarn and knitting needles, cords and laces, lace, elastic, zippers, bobbins, threads, buttons, sewing tools, embroidery hoops, ribbons, fasteners, leather, felt, patterns.

Containers

Mason jars, tins (non-holiday) plastic organizer boxes, Rubbermaid tubs, jewelry boxes, buckets, cigar boxes, film canisters.

Craft Supplies

Fake flowers, doll making supplies, candle wax, soap/candy molds, pipe cleaners, googley eyes, pom-poms, craft foam, marbles, pins, board games/puzzles, beads, jewelry, magnets, corks, masks and small costume elements, seashells.

Office Supplies

Post-its, thumbtacks, staples, paperclips, writing utensils, scissors, hole-punches, staplers, tape dispensers, transparencies, sheet protectors, labels, envelopes, tape, notebooks, clipboards, and select 3-ring binders (1.5" or less, please email to see if we can accept them).

Party/Gift Supplies

Tissue paper, greeting cards (unused), party decorations, ribbons, bows, rolls of gift-wrap, gift-bags, bubble wrap.

Paper

Copy paper, rolls of paper, construction paper, cardstick, art paper and pads, origami paper, posters.

Framing/Signage

Foam core, poster board, mat board, modern or fancy picture frames, old signs, plexiglass, silkscreens, vinyl lettering, hanging hardware, canvases (painted or new), easels.

Architecture/Interior Design

Wallpaper, mosaic tiles (specifically for mosaics, not leftover household tile), paint swatches, mirrors, shelf brackets.

Collage/Scrapbooking Images

Stickers, scrapbook paper, rubber stamps and stamp pads, calendars, playing cards, postcards, National Geographics, select other magazines (please ask for details), maps, old photos.

Metal/Hardware

Wire, keys, nails, screws, bottlecaps, drafting lamps, light kits, X-acto knives, most smaller tools, working lightbulbs.

Donations We DO NOT Accept

Items that are dirty or significantly damaged*

  • Food or perishables
  • Wine Corks
  • Books (unless art/design related)
  • Housewares/furniture/appliances/household linens
  • Dishes
  • Food containers
  • Loose pieces of glass
  • Broken picture frames (missing glass, backing, etc.)
  • Broken glass/ceramics
  • Computer hardware/software
  • VHS tapes, cassette tapes, CDs, or their cases
  • Printers or other electronics
  • Hanging files, file folders or binders over 2" wide
  • Toys
  • Knick-knacks (figurines, ornaments, baskets)
  • House paint
  • Toxic or unidentifiable substances
  • Junk mail
  • Architectural samples
  • Paper towel/toilet paper rolls, egg cartons, styrofoam
  • Most magazines except vintage, National Geographic, and art magazines - please ask
  • Fabric scraps under 1/2 yard (sorry no fabric samples, cut up clothing, household linens or unfinished sewing projects

We will happily provide suggestions for other places to take items that we cannot accept. We appreciate your contribution, and we hope you can find some useful and inspiring materials when you visit our store.
